    How to prepare cereal and milk

    First, you get your favourite bowl and fill it with milk. If you want, you can microwave it for 30-60 seconds. Then you will add the cereal and eat them as they are still crunchy, with your perfect temperature milk (if you do it the wrong way you can't get the milk at your desired temperature and the cereal will get soggy). Enjoy your meal.

    How to prepare grilled pork neck with french fries

    You preheat the air fryer. In the meantime, you season the pork with salt, pepper, chilli, garlic powder and olive oil. You also season the french fries with salt, pepper and olive oil. You put both the pork and the french fries in the air fryer and cook them for 20 minutes; flip them after 10 minutes. You can add any sauce you want. Enjoy your meal.

    How to prepare spicy-cheesy pasta

    First, you start boiling water and preheat a frying pan with butter in it. Fry your onion, add cherry tomatoes, tomato paste and chilli. Then, add the gouda cheese. Put the pasta in the boiling water(don't forget to add salt before). Keep cooking the sauce at low temperature while the pasta boils. Once the pasta is ready, take it out and add the sauce. You can grate some parmesan on top. Enjoy your meal.
